I — About

A long way back to the body.

The body speaks before it speaks — in tension, in held breath, in the small ways we move around what hurts.

This is the work: to listen — until the body answers.

II — The Practice

Two practices, side by side.

Pilates and Feldenkrais — each with its own strength, its own way of working with the body.

Brought together for the modern body: its tensions, its quiet pain, its lost sense of itself.

A class is a small door.
What matters is whether they keep walking through.
